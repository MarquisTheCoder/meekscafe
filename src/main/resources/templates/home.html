We Are Who We Serve

Meeks Cafe is the go-to spot for the best Southern fried ribs in town. From the perfectly crispy exterior to the juicy, tender meat inside, our ribs are a true sensation for any foodie. But that's not all that sets Meeks Cafe apart. As a local business, we are deeply committed to serving and supporting their community. Our warm and friendly atmosphere, coupled with exceptional customer service, makes for an unforgettable dining experience that keeps customers coming back for more. At Meeks Cafe, it's not just about great food, it's about fostering a sense of community and connection.
